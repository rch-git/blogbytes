I got this game for free on Epic Game Store. I heard good things about it, and after playing two Assassin's Creed games back to back, I wanted to play something different. My experience with a walking simulators has not been positive. The much acclaimed Dear Esther was a total let down and Firewatch felt pointless. So, how about a swimming simulator? The visuals looked interesting, and these kind of games tend to be short, so I thought I will try it. Well, I did...and I don't know what to say. I think this game is too deep for me. No pun intended. I have no idea what the story is about. The game did not give the pretentious vibes of Dear Esther, but if there is a story here, I can't figure out what it is. My biggest problem with the game is not the lack of story, but the lack of control and resolution customization. To swim, you have to hold the right mouse button. This got uncomfortable for me very quickly, so much so that I could not play this game for more than 15 minutes or so at a time because holding down right click was causing strain in my right hand. I think this game is meant to be played with a control, which is unfortunate for me because the only games I can play with a controller are racing games. The visuals are fantastic.
